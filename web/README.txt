THE SNAILON INVASION (web version)
==================================

Protect your crew by eliminating the EVIL SNAILONS. May god be with you.

HOW TO PLAY
-----------
Just open index.html in any modern web browser (double-click it).
No installation, no server, nothing to build.

CONTROLS
--------
  Left / Right arrows  - move the salt shaker
  Up / Down arrows     - increase / decrease ship speed (shown on the SPEED meter)
  Space                - fire
  Esc or P             - pause / resume
  B                    - toggle background music
  Click "Play" (or Enter) - start a new game

FEATURES
--------
  - Every level is different. A banner announces each one:
        1  Regular snailons        7  Splitter snailons (split in two when shot)
        2  Exploding snailons      8  Evil snailons (horned, aim slime at you)
        3  GIANT SNAILON boss      9  GIANT SNAILON boss
        4  Fast swarm              10 Matriarchs (pink bow; lay baby snailons)
        5  Armored (2 hits)        11+ Mixed invasions (incl. crowned Kings)
        6  GIANT SNAILON boss
    Each type has its own look (aura + emblem) and behavior.
  - Snailons shoot SLIME globs down at you - dodge them!
  - Killed snailons sometimes drop POWER-UPS - catch them with the ship:
        R (orange) = rapid fire (hold Space)
        S (blue)   = spread shot (3-way)
        ▲ (cyan)   = shield (blocks one hit)
        + (red)    = extra life
  - Every 3rd level a GIANT SNAILON boss appears with a health bar. It slides
    around and spits slime, and speeds up when low on health. Beat it for a
    big score bonus.
  - Explosions, screen shake, background music, snailon trash-talk,
    sound effects, and a persistent high score.
  - 🥫 יש סוד... נסו להקליד משהו טעים באמצע המשחק.

The game speeds up each time you clear a wave. You have 3 lives, and your
best score is saved in the browser between sessions.
